About Cocktiels

The cockatiel (Nymphicus hollandicus), also known as weiro bird, or quarrion, is a small parrot that is a member of its own branch of the cockatoo family endemic to Australia. They are prized as household pets and companion parrots throughout the world and are relatively easy to breed. [2]
The cockatiel is the only member of the genus Nymphicus. It was previously considered a crested parakeet or small cockatoo
